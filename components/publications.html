Analysis Of Driver's Lipothymia

Aravintha Prasath V, et al.

International Journal of Engineering and Advanced Technology

July 2019

IoT EEG Safety

Developed a driver drowsiness monitoring system using EEG sensors embedded in a wearable band. Data is processed via a microcontroller and shared over local Wi-Fi, enabling passengers to view driver alertness. In case of drowsiness, alerts are sent through seat vibration or sound to prevent accidents.

Classification of Abnormalities in ECG using Machine Learning

Aravintha Prasath V, et al.

Journal of Healthcare Engineering

May 2021

IoT ECG MSVM

Proposed an IoT-based health monitoring system for ambulatory ECG recorders using Heart Rate Variability (HRV) and a Modified Support Vector Machine (MSVM) algorithm. The system effectively detects cardiac abnormalities from ECG signals while addressing challenges like memory, battery, and processing load.
